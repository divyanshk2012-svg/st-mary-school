About Us

St. Mary’s Senior Secondary School, Nagina (Bijnor District, Uttar Pradesh) is a co-educational English-medium institution run by the Catholic community. It aims to foster academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development of students.

Affiliation: Recognised by the Government of Uttar Pradesh.

Established: 2004

Medium of Instruction: English

Facilities

  • Spacious classrooms and playground
  • Well-equipped Science and Computer Labs
  • Library and reading room
  • Focus on discipline and character formation
  • Regular co-curricular and sports activities
